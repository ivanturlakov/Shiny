SEO/Content Specialist

Shiny is a full-sevice marketing agency that is looking to expand its service offerings. So we are looking for an experienced SEO marketing professional with a strong entrepreneurial spirit and background in organic search, social media, reputation management, blogging, content strategy, and conversion rate optimization.

Ideally we would like a person with 5+ years of professional experience with excellent communication skills, marketing expertise, strong organizational skills and entrepreneurial spirit.We are looking for an SEO content specialist with experience in the creation, deployment and maintenance of organic search campaigns, advertising campaigns, social media endeavors and content creation.

The ideal candidate will have the following experience:

  • A history of creating organic search optimization strategies to improve traffic, keyword rankings, competitive performance, brand awareness or other marketing KPIs.
  • Coordinated work efforts between web developers, CMS teams and content writers to implement SEO changes on the sites.
  • Collaborated with the internal technology development teams and external agencies in implementing technical backend SEO recommendations to support search engine indexing, crawl and overall SEO strategy.
  • Developed reporting templates used by the SEO team to report on traffic and organic ranking metrics.
  • Created SEO measurement reports on a monthly or quarterly basis using web analytics and SEO keyword ranking software to show the effectiveness of implemented strategies including metrics such as: rankings, visits, goals and conversation rates.
  • Performed keyword research analysis based on analytics, search term popularity metrics, and competitive visibility.
  • Integrated search strategies within mobile, social media, video and other emerging vertical.

Skills/Requirements:

A strong technical background- proficiency in MS Office applications as well as several web analytics packages, buzz monitoring tools and graphics programs.
